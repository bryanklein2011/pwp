About

We host different types of tournament ranging from 6's co-ed to competitive 2's. We host events that range from juniors events to adult outings and event corporate events. Whether you are a weekend warrior, ex-college player, drop-in regular, aspiring pro, or full on volleyball addict, Stone Face Courts  has a league, tournament or clinic to get you on the sand. Check out all we have to offer via our new website.
